Easy To Integrate

dPayID is very easy to integrate with any decentralized/distributed application and takes at most a few lines of code, when using dPayID-SDK. We have documented dPayID-SDK thoroughly and have also created several example dPayID applications for you to go off of during your development journey.

No-Hassle Signups

Who doesn't like an easy signup process? Your users will love the fact that they never have to enter a single email address again or verify a phone number, just to experience a service. A sovereign identity is important to many across the dWeb and their privacy is everything.

An Added Layer Of Security

dPayID is an added layer of security for your users, who will never have to give over their keys within a browser again, considering their keys are saved locally, in an encrypted formatted. This added layer of security brings the likelihood of account hijacking down to a minimum.

Never Store User Data Again

Databases seem to be on their way out of the door, considering P2P-based data distribution is becoming more and more normal throughout today's internet with the growth of blockchains and decentralized applications. dPay is no different except it gives your users a way to earn money from the data they submit. Two major advantages.

Faster Applications

Distributed applications are much faster because in most cases, they don't utilize local databases that can be slowed by large amounts of incoming traffic. Taking this approach will allow you to focus on the important things and not on the boring and pointless things.

24 Hour Community Support

The greatest feature of working within a decentralized community is the community support around a project. We pride ourselves on a growing friendly community who is accepting of everyone and there will always be someone around to help you join the revolution!

We made it easy to integrate dPayID.

It's very easy to implement dPayID and all the developer tools you will ever need are at your disposal to give your users to easiest and best experience when it comes to using your dApp.

1. Generate Your dApp Identity

First you generate your dApp identity on the dPay blockchain. When users of your dApp login via your user interface, you will direct them to your dApp's identity in order to register that user under your decentralized application

2. Get Your dApp Credentials

Ever dApp has a username and an API key, generated alongside the owner, active, posting and memo keys associated with the dApp account itself. You can always access these via your dPayID dashboard.

3. Integrate

Whatever decentralized application your building, integration is easy, as long as you follow along with our developer docs and instructions around using dPayID-SDK. A few lines of code at most, is all that's required.

4. Bring On The Users

Now you're ready to allow users from the dPay/dWeb ecosystem login to your dApp and can now take advantage of all the features within your dApp under that particular user account. How sweet is that?
